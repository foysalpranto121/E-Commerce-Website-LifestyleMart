About LIFESTYLE MART

Bangladesh's Premier Online Shopping Destination - Bringing Quality Fashion to Your Doorstep Since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, LIFESTYLE MART started with a simple mission: to provide Bangladeshis with access to quality fashion and lifestyle products at affordable prices.

What began as a small online store has grown into one of Bangladesh's most trusted eCommerce platforms, serving thousands of customers across the country. We pride ourselves on curating the best collection of clothing, accessories, and lifestyle products from both local and international brands.

Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and innovation has made us a household name in Bangladeshi online retail. We continue to expand our product range and improve our services to meet the evolving needs of our valued customers.
